# Game Design Document

This is a game design document to keep track of the core concept, main features, art style and do not forget what we are building. Because we do not know yet... so I hope this document can help us to keep our idea and vision clear.

## Core Concept

Kakapo parrot was injured when falling down from the tree. Others kapapos are trying to take him to the shaman. The shaman is the only one who can heal Kakapo parrot. The other kapapos have to carry Kakapo parrot on a stretcher and run through the forest to reach the shaman before Kakapo parrot dies. The game ends when the player reaches the shaman or when Kakapo parrot dies.

Player have to avoidance obstacles in the day and navigate throught the map. The game is a strategy, where the player has to collect resources to survive nights. The player has to find berries and wood. Resources needs raises every day, so the player has to find more and more resources to survive.

For night cycle player have to micro management selected kakapos to guard the camp to protect injured kakapo and guard the resourcers. Selected kakapos for the night shift will have debuff for day.

## Main Features
- Day and night cycle
- Resource collection (berries and wood)
- Guard resources and injured kakapo (night cycle) and danger avoidance (day cycle)
- Strategy and decision making

## Technical details
The game using Godot Engine. The game is 3D and using lowpoly art style.

## Art style and aesthetics
The art style of the game is lowpoly. The game is 3D.

## Target platforms and audience
Platforms: PC (Linux, Windows), Web (WebGL)
Audience: Casual gamers, gamejam attendees, people who like cute animals
